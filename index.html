works

Learning Management System

This system was created for an University.
It allows students to
- submit a report and take quizzes
- design their own class schedule
- obtain and visualize teacher reports

and also, teachers can
- design a report for every student
- plan the classes, quizzes and exams
- consolidate a report for the students

Customer Information System

Development and maintenance of a customer management system for an electric power company.
It allows user to create, view, edit, delete information of customers who are under contract.
And also, it can link with other devices to obtain information of usages, fees and payment status.
I was responsible for the design, migration of data and testing plan for the data migration system.

Co-Op Management System

Development Co-Op Management Software which is called Work Link.
It allows to connect the students and the school with the co-op process and I designed and implemented a user-facing interfaces using Vue.js and the MVVM design pattern.
And participating in an Agile team during the planning of the project and the design of the interface.
